About Me

I'm a Craft Maker

who loves creating unique and beautiful handmade crafts. My passion lies in transforming raw materials into stunning pieces of art that tell a story, and I also love creating effective and beautiful user experiences.

With my expertise in art, design, and craftsmanship, I strive to create pieces that not only look good but also resonate with people on a deeper level. I believe that every creation should have a purpose and a story to tell.

When I'm not crafting, you can find me exploring new techniques, attending workshops, or simply enjoying the beauty of nature for inspiration. I'm also love baking or frying things like chin chin and puff puff, and experimenting with new recipes.
